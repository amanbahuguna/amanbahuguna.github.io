About Me

Hello, I am Aman Bahuguna, a User Experience & Communication Designer, currently in the final year of my bachelor course in Design at Symbiosis Institute of Design, Pune, India.

I'm fascinated by the impact of design in enriching user experiences. I have a keen interest in Research, Human Psychology, Human Computer Interaction, Visual Design and Illustration.

I'm an artist at heart and during my free time, enjoy digital photography, compose my own music, write lyrics for songs and perform various gigs as a guitarist in my acoustic band "Altereal".

Education

Courses: User Research, Information Architecture, Interaction Design, User Interface Graphics, Product Interface Design, Game Design, Arduino & Programming, Visual Identity Design, Typography, Elements of Design, Principles of Design and Color Theory.
